NAZARETH'S HISTORY

Ancient History

Prehistoric Era: Archaeological evidence suggests that the area around Nazareth was inhabited as far back as the Bronze Age.

Biblical Times: Nazareth is most famously known as the childhood home of Jesus Christ, and it is mentioned in the New Testament.
The angel Gabriel is said to have announced to Mary that she would bear Jesus here (Luke 1:26-38).

Roman Period: During this time, Nazareth was a small Jewish village. The population was likely quite small, around 400 people.

Possible Ancient Origins: There's evidence of settlements in the Nazareth area dating back to the Bronze Age (around 3300-1200 BCE)!
While we can't be sure of a continuous presence, it hints at a long history for the location.
